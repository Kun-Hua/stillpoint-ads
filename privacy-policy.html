StillPoint ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the StillPoint mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

When you register, we collect your email address via Supabase Authentication to create and manage your account.

B. Focus Session & Usage Data

  • Focus session duration, start/end timestamps, and interruption counts
  • Goal creation, progress, and completion status
  • App settings and preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • In-app events (e.g., ad views, feature interactions) logged to our backend

C. Partner Presence Status

If you use the Partner feature, the App sends a real-time presence heartbeat to our servers approximately every 2 minutes while the App is active.

D. VIP Contact Whitelist

If you use the VIP Filter feature (Premium), contact names and identifiers are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers.

E. Device & Technical Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Timezone
  • Crash reports and error logs (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • App performance metrics (via Firebase Performance)
  • Advertising ID: Google Advertising ID on Android; IDFA via ATT on iOS

3. Special Features & Sensitive Permissions

Notification Listener (Android — VIP Filter)

We do not read, store, transmit, or analyze the content of your notifications. Notification metadata is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never sent to our servers.

Accessibility Service (Android — App Blocking)

We do not capture screenshots, record keystrokes, read text on screen, or collect any data through the Accessibility Service beyond identifying the foreground app name.

Do Not Disturb (DND) Control

During focus sessions, the App may adjust your device's DND settings and system volume. These changes are automatically restored when the session ends. No DND data is transmitted to our servers.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

If you decline ATT, we will serve non-personalized ads. You can change this at any time in your device's Privacy & Security settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide, operate, and improve core app features
  • Enable the Partner feature (real-time presence sharing)
  • Deliver and measure advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • Validate subscription status (via RevenueCat)
  • Monitor app stability and fix crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Analyze aggregate usage trends (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Send relevant push notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: VIP whitelist, Pomodoro settings, and onboarding status are stored only on your device.
  • Remote storage: Account, session, goal, partner, and event data stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS).
  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).

7. Data Retention and Account Deletion

  • Data is retained while your account is active.
  • You may delete your account anytime in the App's settings — all data is immediately and permanently removed.
  • Local data is cleared when you uninstall the App.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data (in-app)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via device settings)
  • Withdraw consent for ATT at any time (iOS Settings)

9. Children's Privacy

StillPoint is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

StillPoint（以下簡稱「我們」）致力於保護您的隱私。本隱私權政策說明我們在您使用 StillPoint 行動應用程式（以下簡稱「本 App」）時，如何收集、使用及保護您的個人資料。

1. 我們收集的資訊

A. 帳號資訊

當您註冊時，我們透過 Supabase 認證服務收集您的電子郵件地址，用於建立及管理帳號。

B. 專注工作階段與使用資料

  • 專注工作階段的時長、開始／結束時間戳記及中斷次數
  • 目標的建立、進度與完成狀態
  • App 設定與偏好（儲存於您的裝置本地）
  • App 內行為事件（例如觀看廣告、使用功能），記錄至我們的後端伺服器

C. 夥伴在線狀態

若您使用夥伴功能，本 App 於啟用狀態下每約 2 分鐘向我們的伺服器傳送一次即時在線狀態。

D. VIP 聯絡人白名單

若您使用 VIP 過濾功能（付費功能），聯絡人資料僅儲存於您的裝置本地，不會上傳至我們的伺服器。

E. 裝置與技術資訊

  • 裝置型號與作業系統版本
  • App 版本與建置號碼
  • 時區
  • 崩潰報告與錯誤日誌（透過 Firebase Crashlytics）
  • App 效能指標（透過 Firebase Performance）
  • 廣告識別碼：Android 收集 Google 廣告 ID；iOS 透過 ATT 框架取得 IDFA

3. 特殊功能與敏感權限說明

通知監聽（Android — VIP 過濾）

我們不會讀取、儲存、傳輸或分析您通知的內容。通知元資料完全在裝置本地即時處理，從不傳送至我們的伺服器。

無障礙服務（Android — 應用程式封鎖）

我們不透過無障礙服務擷取螢幕截圖、記錄按鍵輸入、讀取螢幕上的文字或收集任何資料，僅識別前台應用程式名稱。

勿擾模式（DND）控制

在專注工作階段進行中，本 App 可能調整您裝置的勿擾模式設定與系統音量，工作階段結束後自動還原。所有 DND 資料均不會傳送至我們的伺服器。

App 追蹤透明度（iOS）

若您拒絕 ATT，我們將投放非個人化廣告。您可隨時在裝置的「隱私權與安全性」設定中變更此選擇。
